NAME:
Search on Anna's Archive

SHORT DESCRIPTION (132 characters max):
Quickly search any selected text on Anna's Archive. Right-click any book title or text to find it instantly.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
Search on Anna's Archive is a lightweight extension that lets you search for books, papers, and documents on Anna's Archive directly from any webpage.

How to use:
1. Select any text on a webpage (book title, author name, ISBN, etc.)
2. Right-click to open the context menu
3. Click "Search on Anna's Archive"
4. A new tab opens with your search results

Features:
• Fast and simple - just two clicks
• Works on any webpage
• Lightweight - no background processes
• Privacy-focused - no data collection
• Open source

Perfect for students, researchers, and book lovers who want quick access to Anna's Archive search.
